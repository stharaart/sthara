Designed a aedes,anopheles Mosquito based ornithopter

Studied various types of ornithopter from my area and found deatils about its biology,metabolism,anatomy and movement physics.

designed a double turbine engine(modified from real jet engine ) that can convert the rotor system to flapping mechanism.

designed a mechanism/diagram (fictional) that is ale to generate flapping 8-curvature movement.

wings are designed like that of mosquitoes.

transmission system to convert the energy.

fully functional mosquito ornithopter.
